At Jux, we believe in the power of community. We're building more than just a product; we're cultivating a space where designers and developers come together to shape the future of design systems.

## How to Get Involved

- Join our <a href="https://discord.gg/xa4PR7T9" target="_blank">Discord</a>: Connect with out dev team and other Jux developers.
- Star and join our <a href="https://github.com/jux-io/toolkit" target="_blank">GitHub</a>: Contribute to our open-source projects and discussions.
- Stay in Touch: Have questions or feedback? Email us anytime at [squad@jux.io](mailto:squad@jux.io).

## Your Voice Matters
Your ideas and feedback are invaluable in helping us create the best possible tool for developers. We're always looking for ways to improve, and we'd love to hear from you.
