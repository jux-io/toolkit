At Jux, we believe in the power of community. We're building more than just a product; we're cultivating a space where designers and developers come together to shape the future of design systems.

## How to Get Involved

- Join our <a href="https://discord.gg/BSXsahmyVQ" target="_blank">Discord</a>: Become part of our online community, ask questions, and get help from other Jux users.
- Stay in Touch: Have questions or feedback? Email us anytime at [squad@jux.io](mailto:squad@jux.io).

## Your Voice Matters
Your ideas and feedback are invaluable in helping us create the best possible tool for product designers. We're always looking for ways to improve, and we'd love to hear from you.
